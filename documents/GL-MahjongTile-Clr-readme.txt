- About GL-MahjongTile-Clr
  Colored GL-MahjongTile. This is a web font of SVG color font. It can not be installed on the devices.
  It can be displayed when loaded by FireFox or Microsoft Edge. Not supported by other browsers.


- GL-MahjongTile-Clr key map
  It also supports U+1E000 to U+1E02B Unicode Mahjong Tiles range.

  9 - Back of tile
  / - The joker tile

  (Honer tiles)
  1 - East wind
  2 - South wind
  3 - West wind
  4 - North wind
  5 - White dragon
  6 - Green dragon
  7 - Red dragon
