- About GL-Geomancy
  Font of medieval geomantic figures.
  16 geomantic figures are randomly mapped to all keys of keyboard except Space key. So you can get a geomantic figure when you hit a key on your keyboard randomly.
  U+005C and U+00A5 are same glyph due to the backslash problem.
  In addition, ligatures (liga) are prepared with the names of each figure.
